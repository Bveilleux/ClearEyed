ClearEyed

Concussion recovery designed just for you.

ClearEyed gives you a powerful, patterns-based way to track your concussion progress. ClearEyed is a free web application that helps people affected by mild-traumatic brain injuries and concussions to better track symptoms, perform concussion therapy exercises, and communicate with health care providers.

Track Your Progress

ClearEyed offers both a symptom tracker and Physical Therapy tracker! The Symptom Tracker lets you follow along with your progress with a daily log and routine progress visualizations. With the ClearEyed PT Tracker, track and perform your physical therapy while keeping your PT and healthcare team informed. Improve your physical, eye-exercises, and relax therapy exercises in one place.

The ClearEyed Community

Get connected with people like you!!. The ClearEyed Community is a place for people affected by mild-traumatic brain injuries and concussions to connect. It is the perfect place to share recovery tips, exercises, and support to one another!
